My Projects

Dashboard for Google Adwords Analysis

A company uses Google AdWords to generate leads for its service. This project is to analyse Google AdWords spend; enquiry level and project won in order to understand the effectiveness of Google AdWords.

To understand the effectiveness of Google AdWords, the following aspects are identified that can reflect the effectiveness in data term:

  • ROI-return of investment. This is the ratio of new profit based on the cost spent to achieve that profit. Total value of project won through Google AdWords and cost of delivering those project, and Google AdWords cost data are needed to carry out this analysis
  • Enquiry level vs Google AdWords spend - analyse if there is any trend or pattern
  • Enquiry level vs Google AdWords keywords click - analyse if there is any trend or pattern

This project involves the following tasks:

  1. Identify data sources
  2. Data modelling
  3. Data visualization
  4. Analyse result

Equipment Issue Report and Management App

The company's reliance on shared testing equipment for client services is currently hampered by an informal and inefficient fault-reporting process. The existing method, which include a mixture of paper logs, spreadsheets, and verbal communication, lacks structure, accessibility, and accountability. This leads to critical issues including inconsistent Data, Lack of Visibility and Operational Friction.

This Equipment Issue Report and Management App is developed to streamlines the entire process of reporting, tracking and managing testing equipment faults.

The solution involves using the following tools:

  • Excel
  • OneDrive
  • PowerApps
  • PowerBI
